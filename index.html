Team Manipal Racing is Manipal Academy of Higher Education's official Baja SAE team. Primary Objectives and Purposes

=>  Innovate

Conceptualization, Design and Manufacturing of an All-Terrain car within stipulated time to compete in national events every year.

=>  Engineer

The prime aspects for design of the car pertain to the Drive-train, Roll Cage, Suspension, Brakes and Kill Switch mechanisms, onboard diagnostics to monitor the car performance during a race. Students from diverse domains of engineering – Mechanical Engineering, Automobile Engineering, Electrical and Electronics Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering participate in the entire car design, and building exercise. Being a part of TMR inculcates vital professional skills and value addition which make the students industry-ready.

=>  Compete

To compete against nation-wide teams in Dynamic events like Acceleration, Hill Climb, Suspension and Traction, Maneuverability and Endurance. Static event like Costing, Design, Safety and Marketability/Sales Presentation.

About our new venture -
TMR ELECTRIC

eBAJA is an event organized by SAE INDIA. Introduced in 2014, by the former President, Dr APJ Abdul Kalam, it aims to promote greener mobility.

Since 2018, TMR has planned to step into the world of motor propulsion. After a brief discussion with our faculty advisor and TMR’s own network of alumni, we set on to research how the transition to eBAJA would be done.

A separate subsystem for eBAJA was set up, which in the span of 2 years worked on the intricacies of electronic propulsion, and battery management. This year, confident with our work, we have registered TMR-Electric as a separate entity, along with TMR.
